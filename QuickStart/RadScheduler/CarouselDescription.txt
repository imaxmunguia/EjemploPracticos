<html>Telerik <b>RadScheduler</b> is a highly-customizable appointment presentation component that offers rich Outlook®-style functionality. When used with <b>RadReminder</b> and <b>RadDesktopAlert</b> you can add rich scheduling UI to any Windows Forms application and enjoy a lightweight yet highly customizable component. The product delivers swift performance, simplified deployment and limitless customization capabilities and is suitable for use in large-scale enterprise applications.
